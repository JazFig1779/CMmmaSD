About the Instructor

Chris Manley

is an Undefeated Professional Mix Martial Arts Athlete, teacher and business owner living in Adams Mass. He is the head instructor and runs the day to day operations of the Berkshire County Martial Arts Academy. Chris has been training Martial arts consistently for the past 16 years. He has studied in and excelled at Wrestling, Jiu Jitsu, Karate, Kickboxing, Muay thai, Judo and weapons fighting.
