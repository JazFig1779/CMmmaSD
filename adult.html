BRAZILIAN JIU JITSU

Brazilian Jiu Jitsu is the most effective Martial Art for the average person to learn. No matter how big or small, the techniques of BJJ will work for you and one day might even save your life.

Brazilian Jiu Jitsu is a self defense system that uses grappling techniques, leverage and the attacker's aggression to win the fight. The game plan is to close the distance, execute the takedown, achieve a dominate position, and finish the fight with a choke hold or joint lock.

At the Berkshire County Martial Arts Academy our Brazilian Jiu Jitsu Class's run 90min long. They start with a 10 minute warm up consisting of running, push ups, sit ups and stretching. After that, we regroup to learn the techniques of the day from the professor. At the end of class the more experienced students will being "Rolling" or sparing.

Curriculum

Our Self Defense curriculum is made up of 36 street readiness techniques. Form TakeDowns to Sweeps to Submissions you will learn everything you need to know to defend yourself. There full Curriculum is handed to the student in print their first day of class.

Promotions

There are two key component to promotion in BJJ. First the length of time a student has been studying the art. Second, the students knowledge and understanding of the curriculum. After 3 month a new student will be eligible to test for their first stripe on their white belt. The adult student will journey though the belt system over the next few years as they master the art of Jiu Jitsu.
