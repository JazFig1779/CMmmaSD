Kids Brazilian Jiu Jitsu

What is it?

The Children's BJJ Class is a 60 min lesson design to Improve cardio, balance, self defense skills and overall understanding of Martial Arts and how they work. In class we teach everything from Basic Self defense, Stretching, Wrestling , Judo and Body Mechanics.

Curriculum

The Children's Brazilian Jiu Jitsu Curriculum is made up of 33 Self Defense techniques. Form TakeDowns to Sweeps to Submissions your kids will learn what they need to know to defend themselves and excel in martial arts.

The Belt System

The Belt promotions are a great way to reward the child for a job well done. At the end of class the students will receive a pass or fail grade depending on behavior. If child has to be told twice to quite their shenanigans they will not receive a passing grade for the day. For every 20 classes completed with a passing grade your child will be promoted a stripe.
